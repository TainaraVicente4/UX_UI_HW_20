Project Overview

The U.S Department of Education website was in need of a new redesign , so I gave it a clean and strong layout, and case study shows that visual design alone can have a huge impact on the UX of a website. THE PROBLEM: Based on the interviews result of many user, I learned that website contains lot of important information, however its displayed on a unorganized, overwhelming and confusing way. THE SOLUTION: Redesign a clean and strong and less repetitive layout to make navigation easier and not overwhelming. TOOLS: Figma, Miro.
